# Privacy Policy for Marcus Sync

Marcus Sync does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data (Reminders content and Markdown files) remains on your device and is never sent to any server. The app has no network connectivity, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

The app accesses your Apple Reminders solely to sync them to a local Markdown file that you choose. No data leaves your device.
